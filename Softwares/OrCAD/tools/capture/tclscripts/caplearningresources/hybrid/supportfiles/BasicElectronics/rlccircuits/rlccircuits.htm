RLC circuits

This section covers basic RLC circuit and how to simulate these circuits in OrCAD Capture-PSpice.

The three basic, linear, passive lumped analog circuit components are:

  • Resistor (R)
  • Inductor (L)
  • Capacitor (C)

These components  may be combined in the following circuits:

  • RC circuit
  • RL circuit
  • LC circuit
  • RLC circuit

These circuits are called  passive filters because they do not depend on any external power supply and they do not contain active components such as transistors..
