Registration information

To help us support your new product, please register your OrCAD software by filling out the registration card and mailing it to us within 90 days of purchase. Your registration card is inside the sealed package containing your software.

Note: If you enter the registration number when you install the software, the number is always available from the Help menu, under the About command (ALT, H, A).

We want you to get the most out of your software. As a registered customer, you have a staff of trained Technical Support and Application Engineers, Technical Training Instructors, and Customer Service specialists waiting to help you. By registering your software, you receive these benefits:

 
90 days of free technical support, via telephone, fax, email, FTP, and BBS supplied with purchase of a new product.
 
Version changes supplied free of charge for the first 90 days after purchase.
 
Older versions supported for 90 days after the release of a newer version.
 
You may purchase an Extended Support Agreement, which provides one year of technical support, maintenance releases, and version changes.
 
If you do not purchase an Extended Support Agreement, you may purchase product updates individually.
 
For more information about Extended Support Agreements, product updates, and version changes, contact OrCAD Direct customer service.
